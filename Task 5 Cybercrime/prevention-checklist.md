# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

## Cyber Safety Tips for College Students

- Use **strong and unique passwords** for all important accounts like email, banking apps, and social media. Avoid simple passwords such as your name or date of birth.

- Enable **Two-Factor Authentication (2FA)** on important accounts to add an extra layer of security.

- **Never share OTPs, passwords, or PINs** with anyone, even if they claim to be from a bank or official organization.

- Be careful of **phishing emails, SMS, or messages** that ask for personal information or contain suspicious links.

- Avoid using **public Wi-Fi networks** for sensitive activities like online banking or logging into important accounts.

- **UPI Safety Tip:** Always verify the **UPI ID or QR code** before making a payment to ensure you are sending money to the correct person.

- **UPI Safety Tip:** Do not approve **unknown “Collect Requests”** in UPI apps unless you are sure about the request.

- Install apps only from **trusted sources like Google Play Store or Apple App Store** and check reviews before downloading.

- Keep your **phone, laptop, and apps updated** with the latest security updates.

## Reporting Cyber Crime

If you become a victim of cyber fraud, report it immediately:

- **National Cyber Crime Portal:** https://cybercrime.gov.in  
- **Cyber Crime Helpline:** 1930
