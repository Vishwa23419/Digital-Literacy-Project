# Social Media Do’s and Don’ts for College Students

## Do’s

1. Think before posting anything online and consider how it may affect your reputation.
2. Communicate respectfully with others and maintain a polite tone in discussions.
3. Use strong passwords and enable privacy settings on your social media accounts.
4. Share positive and useful content that reflects professionalism.
5. Verify information before sharing to avoid spreading misinformation.

## Don’ts

1. Do not share sensitive personal information such as phone numbers, passwords, or bank details.
2. Do not post offensive, abusive, or harmful comments about others.
3. Do not accept friend requests or messages from unknown people without verifying them.
4. Do not click suspicious links or advertisements that may lead to scams.
5. Do not post content that could damage your reputation in the future.
